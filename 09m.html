Passive activism

on the unintented influence of index-based investment managers

"The Stock Market is Having its Best Start in 21 Years," reported Sounds great. But what does it mean?

Not much for most people - unless this is reflecting an upward trend in the long run. Most people are not really affected by daily market fluctuations. Not only because they have a relatively very small stake in the stock market , but also because an increasing amount of people invests through passive funds that thrive with long-term growth, rather than short-lasting spikes.
